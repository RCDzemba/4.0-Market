Product Warranty Information

Important: Warranty coverage varies by product type and manufacturer. Always refer to the specific warranty documentation included with your purchase.

Warranty Coverage

Bazara Tech Market offers comprehensive warranty support for all products. Most manufacturers provide warranty coverage that protects against defects in materials and workmanship.

Warranty Duration by Product Category

Product Category Standard Warranty Extended Warranty Available
Laptops 1-2 years Yes
Smartphones 1 year Yes
Tablets 1 year Yes
Gaming Consoles 1 year Yes
Accessories 90 days Limited

What's Covered

  • Manufacturing defects
  • Hardware malfunctions
  • Electrical component failures
  • Manufacturer-specific component issues

What's Not Covered

  • Accidental damage
  • Cosmetic wear and tear
  • Water damage
  • Unauthorized modifications
  • Loss or theft

Warranty Claim Process

  1. Contact our customer support at support@bazara.tech
  2. Provide proof of purchase and product serial number
  3. Describe the issue in detail
  4. Receive a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number
  5. Ship the product to our service center

Extended Warranty

We offer extended warranty options for most products. These can be purchased at the time of sale or within 30 days of your original purchase. Extended warranties provide additional coverage beyond the standard manufacturer's warranty.

Manufacturer Warranties

Some manufacturers may have their own specific warranty terms. We recommend checking the documentation that came with your product or visiting the manufacturer's website for complete details.
