Returns Policy

At Bazara, we are committed to ensuring your complete satisfaction with your purchase. We understand that sometimes a product may not meet your expectations, and we've designed our returns policy to be transparent and customer-friendly.

30-Day Satisfaction Guarantee: We offer a hassle-free 30-day return policy for most products from the date of purchase.

Eligibility for Returns

To be eligible for a return, the following conditions must be met:

  • Item must be returned within 30 days of the original purchase date
  • Product must be in its original, unopened packaging
  • All accessories and documentation must be included
  • Item must be in new, unused condition

Non-Returnable Items

Some items cannot be returned due to hygiene or safety reasons:

  • Opened software
  • Personalized or custom-configured products
  • Intimate electronic devices
  • Items marked as final sale

Return Process

To initiate a return, please follow these steps:

  • Contact our customer support team to obtain a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number
  • Pack the item securely in its original packaging
  • Include the RMA number on the outside of the package
  • Ship the item to our returns center using a trackable shipping method

Refund Processing

Upon receiving and inspecting your return:

  • Refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days
  • Original payment method will be credited
  • Shipping costs for returns are the responsibility of the customer unless the item is defective
Defective Items: If you receive a defective product, we will cover return shipping costs and provide a replacement or full refund.

Warranty Information

Manufacturer warranties vary by product. Please refer to the specific warranty information included with your purchase for long-term product protection.
